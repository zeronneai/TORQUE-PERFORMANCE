Privacy Policy — Torque Performance

Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, including your name, email address, and athlete information. Payment transactions are processed securely by Stripe and we do not store credit card details on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to create and manage your account, process bookings and payments, send booking confirmations and session reminders, and allow coaches to view attendance and roster information.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide our services (Stripe for payments, Supabase for data storage, Clerk for authentication).

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS).

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for parents to manage their children's training. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13. A parent or guardian must create and manage the account.

6. Your Rights

You may request to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us at privacy@zeronne.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or in-app notification.
